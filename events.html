Events & Catering

Outdoors

The Sandlot

Our newest addition to 202 Hometown Tacos, The Sandlot, offers one of the biggest outdoor spaces around town. Being able to seat up to 80 people & fit upwards of 150, it's good for just about any kind of event you can think of which is why we have so many of our own. This space has allowed us to connect more with the Bellevue community & host more jam packed karaoke nights, birthday parties, beer pong tournament events, and even local live bands that we host on the weekends. Even if there's no event, just enjoying the space & enjoying happy hour, relaxing by the firepits or competing in Mega Jenga, Cornhole, or Giant Connect Four with a group of friends or family makes The Sandlot a good space for anyone to have fun.
